About Me

Tom Spedding

I am a graphic designer & illustrator from the Hampshire, who has recently graduated in Illustration from Arts University Bournemouth. My work focuses on transforming ideas into striking visuals that communicate clearly and capture attention, whether through promotional graphics, illustrated artwork, or visual identity design. Alongside illustration, I work on graphic design projects including branding, promotional artwork and social media material. I often combine both illustration and graphic design to create my work. By bringing these two disciplines together, i’m able to create work that balances strong and appealing visuals with clear and effective design. This allows me to create work that is not only visually engaging but also purposeful, blending illustration with thoughtful composition, typography, and layout. My portfolio reflects a mix of personal projects and commissioned work, allowing me to explore new ideas while continually refining my design approach. I’m always interested in collaborating with creators or studios looking for unique visual work.
